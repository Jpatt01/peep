I remember hearing Peep crow for the first time when I was pulling out my car from the garage. After I pulled out the car, I saw him on one of the tables outside, where he let out a really pitiful and terrible sounding crow. He was only several months old before he started crowing, and he’s about a year old now. His crow is still terrible sounding, but its just louder now that he’s older.

Another memory I have is him sneaking up behind me when I was walking to check on the other chickens. He snuck up behind me and attacked the back of my ankles with his beak and claws. That ended up giving me a scar and I must wear rainboots when near him from now on.

Jessie Patterson, 2022.
